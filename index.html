IMDB Movie Reviews

The film industry is incredibly diverse and caters to a wide audience. This ranges from small, indie films to massive blockbusters that gross millions of dollars. We were interested to see any kind of relationship that exists between a film's rating score and its genre. We have gathered an extensive set of information from the IMDB website in an attempt to understand two main questions:

       1. What types of films are popular to the general public?
       2. Which genre of film are movie reviewers, both casual and professional, drawn to or favor?


We began by taking a look at total number of films found on IMDB and grouped them together based on which genre they were most associated with. This gave us an idea of which films the public tend to be attracted to. This goes off the assumption that film companies produce movies that they predict will perform well. We then ordered the genres from largest to smallest. We then asked ourselves the question 'How does the popularity of each genre compare to the number of reviews published for a certain genre?'. To answer this, we aggregated each movie within a certain genre, like we did previously, and then calculated amount of reviews each film got within that genre. We then averaged those out to a single number and plotted the results. This demonstrates which genre reviewers leaned towards more.
